Welcome to my website! Please feel free to browse the original art that are in these pages, and please contact me if you have any questions, suggestions, requests or comments! While attending the Art Institute of Cincinnati, I learned the technique of clayboard etching which is also known as scratchboard. It has become my favorite medium and one of my goals is to become a Master in this technique. I spend many hours photographing horses, birds, wildlife, trees, flowers and anything related to nature to use as guides in my work. My works are also available in a wide variety of replicated formats, such as prints on canvas, note cards, standard prints, etc. I can create the perfect piece for any purpose, including: Artistic pieces to decorate your home, office, or place of business or as gifts to special people.
